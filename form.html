The Growing Problem of E-Waste

E-waste is the fastest-growing waste stream worldwide. Improper disposal poses significant environmental risks.

Environmental Hazards: E-waste contains hazardous materials like lead and mercury.

Health Risks: Exposure to these toxins can cause serious health problems.

Resource Depletion: Recycling recovers valuable materials, reducing the need for new mining.

Our E-Waste Recycling Process

Our process maximizes resource recovery and minimizes environmental impact.

1. Collection: E-waste is collected from various sources.
2. Sorting: Items are sorted by type and material.
3. Dismantling: Manual and mechanical dismantling separates components.
4. Processing: Materials are processed for recovery.
